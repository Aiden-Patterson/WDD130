Starting the game

There are a lot of different strategies you can employ when playing Wordle. One of the best ones is to start off with a strong word. Most people agree that the best first word includes a lot of vowels. This helps with later guessing; since you know which vowels you need, that greatly reduces the number of words available. a popular choices include 'AUDIO' and 'ADEIU' as a first guess.

It is also useful to include popular consonants in your guesses, as this can greatly narrow down a lot of possibilities. Words like 'AISLE' and 'STARE' as a first guess.

If you choose to not play in hard mode, a good guessing combination is to guess 'ADEIU' followed by 'STORY' right after. This guessing patter allows you to guess all vowels, as well as some of the most common consonants, which will give off a very strong start if you are willing to sacrifice your first two moves for the setup

Many people have also done statistical studies to discover what word is the best word to start with. Some words that these computers and algorithims have decided on are 'CRANE' 'IRATE' 'LATER' and 'RAISE' as the best starting words. One thing of noter here is thta all of the words have the letters A, E, and R in them.

Just because a word is a good starting word doesn't mean it will always be helpful. Like in the picture below, the player put in the word 'CRANE' but only got all gray letters. These words are designed to try and help you the most in starting out your game

As an added layer of challenge, you could opt to use a different starting word for every time you play. You can still use the strategies listed above, but this adds the extra challenge of not being able to jsut stick to one good word. I personally like doing this, as it adds to the fun of the game and adds just a little more challenge to it.

The rest of the game

Always move around your yellow guesses. Yellow means that the letter is in the final word, but it isn't in the right spot. Keep these letters in your guesses, but move their position around until it turns green

If you arent' playing in hard mode, make your guesses extremely varied. Don't use the same letters in your guess, even if they were a green guess the word before. This allows you to guess even more words a lot faster. In the example below, the player already knows 4 of the 5 letters that they need in order to guess the final word by turn 2. This greatly narrows down the options for what it could be, with 'FRAME' being the final word.

To solve Wordle as efficiently as possible, try words that include the letters E, T, A, I, O, N, S, H, and R; these are the most common letters in English. Another great trick is to begin with words that start with the letters A, B, C, and S. These letters are the most common starting letters in English.
