What is Wordle?

Wordle is a word guessing game. The game is to try and guess a random word within six tries. While this may seem like a daunting task, it is made easier by your previous guesses either confirming or disproving if the letters in the word appear in the word you are trying to guess

When a letter turns gray, that means that the letter does not appear in the word you are trying to guess

When a letter turns yellow, that means that the letter does appear in the word you are trying to guess, but not in that position

When a letter turns green, that means that the letter does appear in that exact spot in the word you are trying to guess

If there are two of the same letters in the word, each letter will change color on it's own. In this example, the word that is being guessed has two of the letter 'b'b in it. because the player entered two of the letter, they each change color based on where the 'b' is supposed to be

If, however, you guess two letters but the final word only has one of those letters, only one will be colored yellow or green and the other one will be gray. This allows you to know that there is only one of that letter used in the word

Everyone recieves the same word to guess every day, and a new word will not be chosen until the next day. This way, everyone who plays the game will always have the same word to try to guess so you can challenge your friends and family to play

Within the settings, there is also an option for 'hard mode.' In this mode, the game will not allow you to make guesses that don't include letters you have already revealed. If we use the 'KEEPS' example from above, we see that one 'E' is colored yellow. If we were playing in hard mode, every guess afterwards would have to use the letter 'E' in it.

The game will also keep track of how many times you have won, and how many guesses it took you to guess each word. This is displayed at the end of the game in a statistics page
